How We Work

Business Analysis

The first stage is the analysis, In this stage we analyze and audit the work in an integrated manner, then we list the strengths and weaknesses of the project, after that we start working on both sides in parallel.

Architecture

The second stage is the architecture, In this stage we build the scheme and distribute tasks perfectly, with an emphasis on giving tasks related to weaknesses to highly qualified developers.

Developement

The third stage is development, In this stage we build and develop the project, Each developer performs his tasks perfectly, focusing on completing the tasks optimally and on time. Then the entire project is reviewed by the team leader and makes sure that the development process is completed.
